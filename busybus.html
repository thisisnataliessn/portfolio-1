Problem

Riders are complaining about not knowing when their bus is arriving to the Washington & State stop. They can no longer assume the bus they see in the distance is theirs because several new bus routes have recently been added.

Solution

Busy Bus is a minimum viable product (a single screen) designed to logically show riders what buses are arriving next to the Washington & State stop. This was my first project completed in the Bloc apprenticeship program.
